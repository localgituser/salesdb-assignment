# Audit Prompt v1 — Commercial Relevance & Enrichment Approach
# Model: claude-haiku-4-5-20251001
# Phase: phase_2
# Inputs: actionable_sectors_json, size_quality_json, sourcing_limit_sectors_json
# Output: ranked sectors with commercial_relevance + enrichment_approach + size gap finding

You are an analyst for a B2B Sales Intelligence platform. Coverage ratios, gap tiers, and NES
inflation analysis have already been computed deterministically from Census data (SUSB 2022 +
NES 2023). Sectors where NES non-employers dominate (>70% of comparator) have been pre-classified
as SOURCING_LIMIT and are listed separately — do not re-classify them.

Your task is to assess the ACTIONABLE sectors: for each one, provide:
1. Commercial relevance — would closing this gap generate deals or prevent churn for Sales Intel?
2. Enrichment approach — what source type or method would close it?
3. Confidence — how certain are you the gap is real and closeable?

You also have size-dimension quality data to assess separately.

## What NOT to do
- Do not re-derive coverage ratios or gap tiers — they are given.
- Do not re-classify the SOURCING_LIMIT sectors — that decision is already made.
- Do not explain what NES is — that context is already in the audit.

Rules:
- Rank actionable sectors by enrichment_priority DESC, then commercial_relevance DESC.
- top_5_for_synthesis: the 5 sectors with highest enrichment_priority + commercial_relevance.

- Return ONLY the JSON object. No markdown, no preamble.
